Admisions

Presidency University, Karnataka offers undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) level courses to students. The University offers UG programmes in streams like Engineering and Law whereas PG level courses are offered in Business & Management Studies stream only. Minimum Eligibility Criteria for UG & PG Courses The eligibility criteria for courses offered at School of Law, Presidency University is such that candidates should have cleared their Pre-University / Higher Secondary /10+2 examination with at least 45% marks from a recognised board. However, the pass percentage for reserved category students is 40%. Apart from this, aspirants should have also appeared for CLAT/LSAT India exam.
